Our Story

WE ARE
WEARVIO

Fashion that speaks before you do.

WEARVIO was born in 2023 in a college dorm in Mumbai. Two friends tired of boring fast-fashion decided to build something that actually hit — a platform where AI meets street culture, drops are always fire, and every piece has a story.

We work with indie designers, up-and-coming streetwear labels, and sustainable manufacturers to bring limited, loud collections always on the pulse of what GenZ wants.

Our AI stylist genuinely learns your taste, tracks trends in real-time, and helps you build fits that make you feel unstoppable. Fashion should be personal — not generic.
